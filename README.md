Namma-Vastra
Problem Statement

Traditional handloom weavers and small saree businesses face many challenges in today’s digital market. Most weavers do not have a proper online platform to showcase their products, connect with customers, or promote their craftsmanship. Many customers are unaware of trending saree designs, weaving techniques, and the stories behind handmade sarees.

Due to the lack of digital exposure:

Local weavers struggle to reach wider audiences.
Customers cannot easily explore authentic handloom products.
Small weaving communities face financial difficulties.
Traditional weaving culture is slowly losing visibility.

There is a need for a user-friendly mobile application that can digitally support saree weavers, display saree collections, provide trend insights, and create awareness about handloom culture.

Detailed Description

Namma-Vastra is an Android-based application developed to support saree weavers and saree enthusiasts by creating a digital platform for showcasing traditional and modern sarees. The application helps users explore saree collections, view trending designs, understand weaving traditions, and estimate material costs.

The app acts as a bridge between traditional weaving communities and modern technology. It allows users to upload saree images, browse weaving galleries, and learn about different saree styles and craftsmanship.

The application is designed with a simple and elegant user interface using XML layouts and Java programming in Android Studio. Firebase integration is used for storing images and managing application data.
